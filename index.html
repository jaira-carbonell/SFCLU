Introduction to San Fernando, La Union

The City of San Fernando is the first city created in the province of La Union by virtue of Republic Act 8509, sponsored by the Congressman Victor F. Ortega in the House of Representatives, signed by President Fidel V. Ramos on February 13, 1998 and was ratified by a plebiscite on March 20, 1998. The City of San Fernando is situated in the mountainous area at almost the mid-section of La Union along the China Sea. It was founded in 1786 and made the “cabecera” or provincial capital on the same day that La Union became province in 1850. Feel free to recommend some tourist spots in La Union that you’ve visited!

Make your way to La Union’s tourist spots, dubbed as the surfing capital of the northern Philippines. Explore nature, history, and culture rolled into one in this beautiful province. La Union is one of the best surfing spots in the country. Every summer, thousands of local and foreign visitors troop to its beaches to ride its magnificent waves and enjoy the scenery. But La Union is way more than that. Unknown to many, La Union hides numerous tourist attractions on its sleeves. Take note.
